Unusual activity recognition from video surveillance is an effective research area for computer image processing and deep learning. With visual employment, human activities can be observed in sensitive and public places such as bus stations, railway stations, airports, banks, shopping malls, schools and colleges, car parks, roads, etc. Preventing terrorism, theft, accidents and illegal parking, vandalism, fighting, chain snatching, crime, and other suspicious activities. It is very difficult to view public places on an ongoing basis, so there is a need for smart video surveillance that can monitor people's activities in real-time and classify them according to common and unusual activities, and may issue a warning.
